The FishEye component can be set to expand to the right, left or from the centre. In conjunction, you can specify if you want the menu to expand up or down.

  • The first FishEye opens to the right and expands up with a 200% zoom.
  • The second FishEye opens to the left and expands down with a 150% zoom. We change the component's alignment by putting it into a containing div and changing the alignment.
  • The third FishEye opens from the centre and expands up with a 250% zoom. Again, we place the component in a div to specify the alignment.
